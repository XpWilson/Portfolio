Downfall

Trailer for Downfall

Overview

Downfall was my Capstone project for Full Sail University. It is based on the indie game Downwell by Ojiro Fumoto & Devolver Digital. I made this along with every designer of my team, Capstone’s Finest.

It is a vertical-style 2D platformer game in which the player must reach the bottom of each level to progress. Along the way, there are enemies to defeat, from bats to worms to even killer robots.

Features

In Downfall there are two ways to attack, stomping and shooting enemies.

Shooting

Shooting enemies is simple, press the spacebar in the air and a projectile gets shot below the player. Any enemies that get hit by this get destroyed. Keep in mind there is a limit to how much you can shoot at a time.

Stomping

Stomping enemies is also easy. It’s as simple as landing on top of an enemy. After that, they get destroyed. If you can continuously stomp enemies without touching the ground a combo will show up, giving you rewards.

In order to regain ammo just land on the ground or stomp on an enemy.

Gems

Gems are the currency of Downfall, gems appear when defeating enemies, clearing gameplay setups, or exploring secret areas. Collecting many gems in a short timeframe unlocks the Gem High ability. Which then allows the player to increase their speed.

Merchants are shop keepers located in every level, by trading gems you are able to get goodies such as healing items or ammo increases.

Dashing

Double tapping the movement keys in any cardinal direction allows you to dash in that direction. Using this mechanic in the air will cost the player 1 ammo point.

Color Shift

At any point the player is able to change the color of the background to red, green or blue (RGB) in order to clear obstacles. Platforms of the same color as the background will be considered non-existent making the player able to phase through objects.
